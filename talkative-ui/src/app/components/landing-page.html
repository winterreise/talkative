What is Talkative?

It's your conversation sidekick.

At Talkative, we believe in good conversation. However, spontaneity can be a tricky thing sometimes -- maybe you have a case of first date jitters, or you're at a party where you don't know anyone. We've all been there, right?

That's where our app comes into play. Talkative sends you texts filled with the news, entertainment and quirky facts you're interested in talking about, as frequently as you want.

It's versatile.

Sure, we've got a sweet blend of content that's always fresh and sourced daily from Reddit. But icebreakers aren't our only specialty -- Talkative is useful pretty much any time you need a reason to check your phone. Go ahead, save us under a decoy name. We won't tell if you won't.

It's fun.

Less dreading awkward silences, more talking; less routine, more discovery. Those sound like winning combinations to us. Take Talkative with you in your pocket and jumpstart conversations around you today!

How It Works

Quick Setup

Sign up with your Google Account. When you securely provide us with the phone number you want your Talkative texts sent to, we're off to the races.

Custom Content

Have it your way. Want more thought-provoking facts and less politics? Done. Don't want us to blow up your phone every five minutes? We've got that covered, too.

Talk Away

Sparking conversations was never this easy. Once you've got the conversational ball rolling, you can disable and re-enable Talkative prompts at any time.
